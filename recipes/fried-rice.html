Chicken & Egg Fried Rice

Description

This is a recipe for the dish that will most likely be my dinner most often.

Again, this is a dish that is high in protein to compliment my fitness journey.

This recipe is also low in salt, loaded in veggies, and has healthy carbs.

A more detailed blog post is also available.

Ingredients

  • Toasted Sesame Oil
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Onion, Garlic, Peppers, Carrots
  • Ground Ginger
  • Soy Sauce
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Eggs
  • Frozen Veggies
  • Cooked Rice (White or Brown)

Steps

  1. In a pan, saute the chicken for 15-20 minutes until brown on edges. Remove chicken from pan and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, saute onions and garlic for 5 minutes. After 5 minute add in peppers and carrots. Saute for an additional 5 minutes.
  3. Push the veggie mixture to one side of the pan. In aa small bowl, whisk together eggs until combined. Add eggs to the pan and scramble on the empty side of the pan.
  4. Once eggs are cooked (about 1-2 minutes), stir together with veggie mixture.
  5. Add pepper, ginger, red pepper flakes, toasted sesame oil, soy sauce, thawed peas, cooked rice and cooked chicken back to the pot. Stir over low heat until combined and warm.
