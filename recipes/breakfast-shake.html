Breakfast Protein Shake

Description

This is a recipe for a breakfast shake I will be consuming throughout the 2022-23 school year as a sophomore.

This breakfast shake is high in calories and protein to help accelerate my growth in the gym.

This shake is made with a blender for quick production and consumption.

Ingredients

Note that italicized text means the component is optional

  • 1.5 Frozen Bananas
  • 5 Frozen Dates
  • A spoonful of almond butter
  • Handful of oats
  • A scoop and a half of whey protein
  • Good amount of milk

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients in a sufficiently large and powerful blender
  2. Blend well
  3. Take out the cup and Voila!
